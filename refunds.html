Refund & Delivery Policy

Each sea shanty created by The Shanty Shack is written and produced as a personalised digital product based on the information provided in your order.

Refunds

Because every song is custom-made, refunds are available only before production begins.

If you change your mind shortly after placing an order and work has not yet started on your shanty, you may request a full refund.

Once writing or production has begun, refunds are generally not available due to the personalised nature of the work.

However, if there is a genuine issue with your order, please contact us and we will always do our best to resolve the problem.

Delivery

Completed sea shanties are delivered digitally via email.

Most orders are completed within 3–5 days. During busy periods delivery may take slightly longer.

If you have not received your shanty within 7 days, please contact us and we will investigate immediately.
