Dockside Drinking Song Style

Peggy the parrot rules the harbour taverns with a squawk and a swagger. In this dockside drinking shanty the crew roar along as Peggy leads the chorus from the captain’s wheel, louder than any sailor in the port.

Listen to the example

Dockside drinking songs are the rowdiest of sea shanties — built for taverns, ale-soaked tables and roaring chorus lines. Expect stomping rhythms, boisterous singing and a chorus everyone can shout along to.
