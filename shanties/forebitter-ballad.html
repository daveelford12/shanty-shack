Forebitter Ballad Shanty Style

Old Jasper the harbour cat has watched a thousand ships come and go from his perch on the dockside crates. In this forebitter-style ballad the crew sing of Jasper’s quiet wisdom and the many voyages he’s seen beneath the lantern glow of the port.

Listen to the example

Forebitters were songs sailors sang during their rest hours while gathered near the forebitts of the ship. These shanties are slower and more melodic, telling stories of adventure, longing and life at sea.
