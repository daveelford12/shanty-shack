Capstan Shanty Style

Monty the sausage dog may be small, but aboard the Sofa Fleet he stands tall. In this capstan-style shanty the crew sing of Monty’s bravery as he surveys the living room seas and guards the biscuit tin like a true admiral.

Listen to the example

Capstan shanties were traditionally used for heavy ship work and feature strong rhythm, powerful harmony refrains and a steady marching pulse — perfect for heroic pet stories.
