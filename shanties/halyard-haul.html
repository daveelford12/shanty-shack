Halyard Haul Shanty Style

Captain Whiskers — terror of the tuna tin — prowls the galley like a pirate captain hunting treasure. In this halyard haul shanty the crew shout the rhythm as Whiskers launches daring raids on the ship’s provisions, leaving empty tins and baffled sailors in his wake.

Listen to the example

Halyard shanties were sung while sailors hauled ropes to raise sails high above the deck. These songs feature strong call-and-response rhythms that match the pulling motion of the crew, creating powerful and energetic choruses.
