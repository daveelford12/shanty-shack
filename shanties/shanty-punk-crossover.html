Shanty Punk Crossover Style

Bruno the Bulldog Stormbreaker doesn’t follow orders — he makes them. With a cutlass clenched between his teeth and the wind howling across the deck, Bruno leads the crew into chaos and chorus alike. In this shanty punk crossover the sailors stomp the boards, shout the refrain and follow Bruno wherever the storm may take them.

Listen to the example

Shanty punk blends traditional sea shanty vocals with the rebellious energy of modern folk punk. Expect stomping rhythms, rough-edged harmonies and loud choruses that feel more like a dockside riot than a quiet work song.
